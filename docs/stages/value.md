# Value

**Stage 3 of 5** • Personal Capability

!!! abstract "Core Question"
    **Can I independently create outcomes?**

## Overview

The Value stage represents the transition from supervised execution to independent outcome creation. Practitioners at this stage don't just complete tasks—they deliver measurable value without oversight. They earn trust through results.

## Output

**Measurable Outcomes** — Business value, solved problems, achieved goals.

## Characteristics

- Works independently
- Makes sound decisions
- Delivers measurable outcomes
- Earns trust through results
- Solves problems autonomously

## What "Value" Looks Like

### Product Builder

- Ships products without supervision
- Drives measurable business outcomes
- Makes sound product decisions
- Owns the full product lifecycle

### Software Engineer

- Owns technical decisions
- Delivers complex features end-to-end
- Mentors junior engineers informally
- Trusted to work independently

## Evidence of Value Creation

- Delivers outcomes, not just outputs
- Requires minimal supervision
- Makes decisions others trust
- Has track record of success
- Solves novel problems effectively

## The Outcome vs Output Distinction

| Output (Execute) | Outcome (Value) |
|------------------|-----------------|
| Shipped a feature | Increased user engagement 25% |
| Wrote documentation | Reduced support tickets 40% |
| Built an API | Enabled 10 new integrations |
| Completed the project | Delivered business value |

!!! tip "Key Shift"
    At Execute, you're measured by completing work. At Value, you're measured by the **impact** of that work.

## Why This Stage Matters

Value is often where many practitioners plateau. They become reliable executors but don't progress to multiplying their impact through others.

This is a **stable equilibrium**—a practitioner can have a successful career at Value without ever reaching Enable or Replicate.

## Common Pitfalls

!!! warning "Comfort Zone"
    Staying in individual contributor mode when capable of more. Some choose this; others get stuck.

!!! warning "Hero Culture"
    Over-indexing on personal delivery rather than team success. This limits future growth.

!!! warning "Measuring Activity"
    Confusing busyness with value. Hours worked ≠ outcomes delivered.

## Transition to Enable

A practitioner is ready to transition to [Enable](enable.md) when they:

- Have proven ability to deliver value independently
- Begin informally mentoring others
- See opportunities to multiply through people
- Understand that their ceiling is limited as an individual

## Key Insight

> Value is where expertise becomes trusted. But individual excellence has limits. The next stage asks: can you multiply that value through others?

In the AI era, Value becomes easier to achieve earlier in a career. AI tools can augment judgment and execution. But the fundamental question remains: can you create outcomes that matter?
