    Product Builder Profile¶

    Domain: Product

    Description: LEVER interpretation for Product Builders—individuals who own end-to-end product outcomes.

    Overview¶

    The Product Builder role represents the evolution of product management in the AI era. Unlike traditional PMs who coordinate specialists, Product Builders own vision, design, development, and delivery.

    Product Builder Definition

    A Product Builder is a mini solo founder inside an enterprise—owning outcomes, not just requirements.

    Stage Interpretations¶

    Learn → Learner¶

    Description: Acquiring product, technology, and business knowledge.

    Evidence:

    • Completes relevant courses and certifications
    • Studies successful products and case studies
    • Learns technical fundamentals (coding, AI, tools)
    • Understands business models and metrics

    Examples:

    • Takes product management courses
    • Learns to use AI coding assistants
    • Studies customer discovery techniques
    • Completes technical bootcamps

    Execute → Practitioner¶

    Description: Building prototypes and MVPs with guidance.

    Evidence:

    • Creates functional prototypes
    • Uses AI tools to accelerate development
    • Completes features with supervision
    • Participates in product delivery

    Examples:

    • Builds an internal tool with AI assistance
    • Ships a feature to production
    • Conducts user interviews
    • Creates product documentation

    Value → Builder¶

    Description: Independently delivering products that create business value.

    Evidence:

    • Ships products without supervision
    • Drives measurable business outcomes
    • Makes sound product decisions
    • Owns the full product lifecycle

    Examples:

    • Launches a product that acquires users
    • Improves key metrics through product changes
    • Manages a product roadmap independently
    • Handles stakeholder communication autonomously

    Enable → Leader¶

    Description: Developing other Product Builders.

    Evidence:

    • Mentors junior builders
    • Creates learning opportunities
    • Builds high-performing teams
    • Establishes product practices

    Examples:

    • Mentors a practitioner to builder level
    • Runs product craft sessions
    • Defines team product processes
    • Creates onboarding programs

    Replicate → Multiplier¶

    Description: Creating systems that scale product capability.

    Evidence:

    • Creates reusable frameworks and playbooks
    • Builds AI-powered product tools
    • Defines organizational standards
    • Establishes product communities

    Examples:

    • Creates a product requirements AI agent
    • Writes a product playbook adopted by teams
    • Establishes a product guild or community
    • Builds a platform used by other builders

    Career Progression¶

    Stage Title Focus
    Learn Associate Product Builder Learning fundamentals
    Execute Product Builder I Shipping with guidance
    Value Product Builder II Independent delivery
    Enable Senior Product Builder Team development
    Replicate Principal Product Builder System creation

    Competency Areas¶

    Product Discovery¶

    • Customer research
    • Problem identification
    • Opportunity sizing
    • Solution validation

    Product Delivery¶

    • Roadmap management
    • Technical implementation
    • Quality assurance
    • Launch execution

    Business Acumen¶

    • Metric definition
    • Revenue understanding
    • Stakeholder management
    • Strategic thinking

    Technical Skills¶

    • AI/ML fundamentals
    • Coding basics
    • Data analysis
    • System design

    Assessment Criteria¶

    Learn → Execute¶

    • [ ] Completed foundational training
    • [ ] Understands product development lifecycle
    • [ ] Can use basic tools independently
    • [ ] Ready to contribute with guidance

    Execute → Value¶

    • [ ] Shipped multiple features successfully
    • [ ] Demonstrates sound judgment
    • [ ] Works effectively with minimal oversight
    • [ ] Delivers measurable outcomes

    Value → Enable¶

    • [ ] Consistent track record of value creation
    • [ ] Shows interest in developing others
    • [ ] Demonstrates leadership behaviors
    • [ ] Can articulate product principles

    Enable → Replicate¶

    • [ ] Successfully developed multiple team members
    • [ ] Created repeatable approaches
    • [ ] Sees opportunities for systemic improvement
    • [ ] Has vision for scaled impact

    Relationship to PBMM¶

    This profile is the foundation for the Product Builder Maturity Model (PBMM), which provides detailed assessment criteria and benchmarks for each stage.

    See PBMM on ProductBuildersHQ for the full maturity model.
